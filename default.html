I AM A WEB DESIGNER

I’m a Web Designer and Full‑Stack Developer who builds websites end‑to‑end—from design to development. My focus isn’t just on creating “good‑looking UI”; I build websites that are responsive, fast, user‑friendly, and aligned with business goals such as leads, sales, and branding. I have a strong sense of UI/UX, layout structure, typography, colors, and spacing, which helps my designs look clean and professional.
